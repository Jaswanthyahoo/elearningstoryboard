Our Methodology

  •   On line theory classes will be conducted through WebEX / GoToMeeting as per the schedule given in advance. Registered students should make themselves available as per the schedule.


  •   Workshops and Exercises should be done by the students on their own. Queries if any should be mailed to the instructor well in advance of the next session. Instructor shall clarify all the queries in the next session.


  •  Students are supposed to use Academic Software License for their workshops and exercises.


  •  All the registered students should submit a soft copy of the report of all the exercises done by them for scrutiny by the instructor.


  •   Feedback form is filled up by the students at the end of the course


  •  Certificates shall be couriered to the students
