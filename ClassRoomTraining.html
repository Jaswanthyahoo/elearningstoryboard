  Our Vision

ARTEM offers theoretical and software courses for career progression in the field of design and analysis. Courses have been designed by the industrial experts and academicians. Courses are planned to cover a broad range of topics from the basic physical concepts to advanced modeling techniques through scheduled classes. Training solutions are designed to provide you with both the comprehensive and subject-specific knowledge you need to effectively apply to solve your engineering problems. Our strength is to make you expertise conforming to industry standards with the academic knowledge gained.

Who should do the basics course?

Who should do the basics course? This course is for pretty much anybody who is interested in engineering. We teach you basics of CAE such that you can use any modeling and analysis software to produce results by using the fundamentals of FEA principles and to make sense out of them.

Why should I do it? Suppose you have access to the software and even learnt the basics of FEA, but that is theoretical knowledge. Our pedagogy is pragmatic approach. In addition to elementary teaching about the subject, you will also learn the idiosyncrasies of the tool. We will teach you to examine the data methodically, which will enable you or your clients to design better. We have years of experience in applying thought to perfect the output so that you don’t have to start from scratch. After all, we are humans; we build upon knowledge from experience; and we have plenty of it.

Theoretical course content will be same as academic syllabus for long term with the industry usage and live examples.
